About Me

Hello! I'm an FPGA Engineer with a strong background in hardware development and verification. I've had the privilege of working with Vicharak Computers LLP and GalaxEye Space LTD, where I contributed to the development of Single Board Computers and data acquisition architectures for CMOS Sensors.
I have a deep understanding of Hardware Description Languages, particularly Verilog HDL, and am proficient in using developer tools such as Xilinx Vivado, Vitis IDE, and Multisim.
